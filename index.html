| About Us

The pursuit of increasing our capabilities and developing ourselves is what prompted us to shift from an individual institution to an International Supply Support Company O.P.C is a continuation of the Bahobeshi Trading and customs clearance Est, Which is considered one of the first agent in the field of customs clearance, which was established in 1395 AH, more than four decades ago, during that we served many national and investment companies and factories operate on various commercial , industrial and contracting activities. We continue to offer our services to our customers' satisfaction and appreciation for their keenness to continuously develop our work mechanisms to offer total solution to achieve maximum speed and workmanship Its existence and our constant motto is that we are distinguished by our customers.

| Services

Custom Clearance

We provide customs clearance services from Jeddah Islamic Port

Transport Service

We provide our clients with the most efficient transportation services that delivers their cargo in the shortest possible period of time.

Storage Services

When it is difficult for the client receive the goods at the same time it can be stored in storage yards until the appropriate time.

Consulting

We provide customs clearance consultancy that focuses on our client’s most critical issues and/or opportunities.



| Three Easy Steps to Import to Saudi Arabia

1
First Step

Provide us with an online/electronic authorization via Saudi Customs website;License Number: 2643,Name:International Supply Support Company O.P.C.

2
Second Step

Deliver the required documents to our office, such as:

1. Commercial Invoice.

2. Bill of Lading.

* Any supporting documents (special documents if required, such as SASO or COA).

3
Third Step

Pay customs duty when shipment is cleared.
